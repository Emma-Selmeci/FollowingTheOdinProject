Terraforming Mars

Description

Terraforming Mars is a thematic engine-building game about the most ambitious project undertaken by mankind - turning Mars into a habitable planet.

The game is mostly competitive, direct PvP elements are limited

Positives

  • Many different paths to victory
  • High replayability
  • Enthusiastic modding community

Round

  1. Players draw and buy project cards
  2. Players play actions in sequence
  3. Players produce money, steel, titanium, plants, energy and heat
