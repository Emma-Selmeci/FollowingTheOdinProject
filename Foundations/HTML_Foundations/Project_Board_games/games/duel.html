7 Wonders Duel

Description

7 Wonder Duel is a duel game, where two players compete to build the mightiest city.

The game can be won in 3 different ways, and my partner knows how to do any of these.

Positives

  • Very competitive
  • Short and tense gameplay
  • One of the best 2-player games available

Round

  1. Players prepare the cards for the corresponding age
  2. Players choose and play cards in sequence
