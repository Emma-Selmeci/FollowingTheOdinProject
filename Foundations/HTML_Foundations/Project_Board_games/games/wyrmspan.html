Wyrmspan

Description

Wyrmspan is an engine-building game, where players house dragons in caves and use their abilities to collect points.

The game is mostly competitive, players can hinder each other, but no direct attacks are possible

Positives

  • Gorgeous design
  • Exciting booklet describing all the different dragons
  • Gold resource tokens look like butter OMG

Round

  1. Players refresh the card bank, gain 6 coins and 1 egg
  2. Players play actions in sequence
  3. Players score victory points for reaching goals
